About Meazura AI

Meazura AI is an intelligent digital companion designed specifically for the modern tailor and fashion enthusiast. Built by Codedhans, it combines the precision of traditional garment construction with the speed of artificial intelligence.

Whether you are trying to calculate fabric yardage, exploring vintage shirt styles, or looking for advice on custom palazzo pants, Meazura AI provides real-time solutions to keep your creative workflow seamless.

Codedhans

is a creative intersection where fashion craftsmanship meets digital innovation. We specialize in custom tailoring and vintage-inspired apparel, bridging the gap between generations through timeless style.

Our goal is to empower users with AI-driven styling tools and high-quality digital products that simplify the design process.

How to Use the App

  • 01. Consult the AI: Tap the AI Assistant button to ask questions about garment fitting, fabric types, or design inspiration.
  • 02. Build Your Gallery: Upload photos of your finished works or design sketches. Your collection is saved locally so you can showcase your portfolio anytime.
  • 03. Manage Your Styles: Use the Gallery overlay to scroll through your vintage collection, preview images in full-screen, or delete old styles to make room for new ones.
  • 04. Stay Connected: Use the navigation links to find our contact details for custom tailoring commissions or technical support.

Privacy Policy

At Meazura AI, your privacy is our priority. We only store image data locally on your device's browser to ensure that your style choices remain private to you.

We do not sell your personal data. Any information shared during AI chat interactions is used solely to provide tailored fashion advice and is processed securely via our encrypted endpoints.
